Rock • Paper • Scissors • Lizard • Spock

Project description

In this project, I designed and developed an exciting and interactive rock, paper, scissors, lizard, and Spock game (as seen on The Big Bang Theory TV show) that challenges players to test their luck and strategy against a computer opponent. Using HTML for structure, CSS for a modern look, and JavaScript to generate the computer's choice and compare the results, I created a seamless and engaging user experience. Players can win, lose, or tie, and scores are tracked at the bottom to keep track of progress. With a keen eye for design and a passion for interactive experiences, I created a fun and engaging game that is sure to keep players coming back for more..
